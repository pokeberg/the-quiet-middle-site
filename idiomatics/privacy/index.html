Idiomatics

Privacy Policy

Idiomatics is designed to be a calm, lightweight experience. Your privacy is respected by default.

Data Collection

Idiomatics does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does not require account creation and does not track users across apps or websites.

Game Data

Any progress, scores, or preferences are stored locally on your device. If Game Center is enabled, scores may be submitted to Apple’s Game Center services according to Apple’s own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Idiomatics uses Apple-provided services only, such as Game Center. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be made available at this URL.
